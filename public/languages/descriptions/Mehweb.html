Mehweb

Contributed by Michael Daniel and Majsarat Musaeva.

The data were initially gathered in 2018 and then some changes have been made in 2024. The Russian version of the questionnaire was used for elicitation.

How to cite

Daniel, Michael and Majsarat Musaeva. 2024. Bivalent patterns in Mehweb. In: Say, Sergey (ed.). BivalTyp: Typological database of bivalent verbs and their encoding frames. (Data first published on January 21, 2024; last revised on March 19, 2025.) (Available online at https://www.bivaltyp.info, Accessed on )

Basic info

  • Coordinates: 42.25, 47.05.
  • Genealogy (as given in WALS). Family: Nakh-Daghestanian, genus: Dargwic.
  • Macro-area: West Asia and the Caucasus.
