Udi

Contributed by Timur Maisak (Academia.edu profile)

The data were collected in 2018-2019 using the Russian version of the questionnaire. The data come from the Nidzh dialect of Udi. The translations were provided by Rafik Danakari. Corina van Willigen, Elmina Danakari and Vladislav Dabakov contributed to the collection and analysis of the data.

How to cite

Maisak, Timur. 2025. Bivalent patterns in Udi. In: Say, Sergey (ed.). BivalTyp: Typological database of bivalent verbs and their encoding frames. (Data first published on May 25, 2025; last revised on October 22, 2025.) (Available online at https://bivaltyp.info, Accessed on .)

Basic info

  • Coordinates: 40.9, 47.72.
  • Genealogy (as given in WALS). Family: Nakh-Daghestanian, genus: Lezgic.
  • Macro-area: West Asia and the Caucasus.
