Kurmanji (Northern Kurdish)

Contributed by Nikita Kuzin (Academia.edu profile)

Nikita Kuzin obtained the data in 2024 from four different speakers. English and German were used as languages of elicitation.

How to cite

Kuzin, Nikita. 2024. Bivalent patterns in Kurmanji (Northern Kurdish). In: Say, Sergey (ed.). BivalTyp: Typological database of bivalent verbs and their encoding frames. (Data first published on November 19, 2024; last revised on February 18, 2025.) (Available online at https://bivaltyp.info, Accessed on )

Basic info

  • Coordinates: 37.89, 41.13.
  • Genealogy (as given in WALS). Family: Indo-European, genus: Iranian.
  • Macro-area: West Asia and the Caucasus.
