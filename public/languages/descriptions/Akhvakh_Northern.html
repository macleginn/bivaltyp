Northern Akhvakh

Contributed by Indira Abdulaeva and Gilles Authier (Academia.edu profile).

The data were collected in 2023 using the Russian version of the questionnaire.

How to cite

Abdulaeva, Indira and Gilles Authier. 2023. Bivalent patterns in Northern Akhvakh. In: Say, Sergey (ed.). BivalTyp: Typological database of bivalent verbs and their encoding frames. (Data first published on December 15, 2023; last revised on June 6, 2024.) (Available online at https://www.bivaltyp.info, Accessed on .)

Basic info

  • Coordinates: 42.4, 46.3.
  • Genealogy (as given in WALS). Family: Nakh-Daghestanian, genus: Avar-Andic-Tsezic.
  • Macro-area: West Asia and the Caucasus.
