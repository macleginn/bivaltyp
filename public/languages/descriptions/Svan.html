Svan

Contributed by Alexander Rostovtsev-Popiel (Academia.edu profile)

The data were gathered in 2023. The language of elicitation was Georgian.

How to cite

Rostovtsev-Popiel, Alexander. 2023. Bivalent patterns in Svan. In: Say, Sergey (ed.). BivalTyp: Typological database of bivalent verbs and their encoding frames. (Data first published on October 13, 2023; last revised on October 13, 2023.) (Available online at https://www.bivaltyp.info, Accessed on )

Basic info

  • Coordinates: 42.91, 42.59.
  • Genealogy (as given in WALS). Family: Kartvelian, genus: Kartvelian.
  • Macro-area: West Asia and the Caucasus.
