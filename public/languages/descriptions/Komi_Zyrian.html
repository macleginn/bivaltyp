Komi-Zyrian

Contributed by Ekaterina Sergeeva (Academia.edu profile).

Translations were elicited from two native speakers of Komi-Zyrian in Syktyvkar in 2013, using the Russian version of the questionnaire.

How to cite

Sergeeva, Ekaterina. 2025. Bivalent patterns in Komi-Zyrian. In: Say, Sergey (ed.). BivalTyp: Typological database of bivalent verbs and their encoding frames. (Data first published on May 26, 2025; last revised on May 26, 2025.) (Available online at https://bivaltyp.info, Accessed on .)

Further details can be found in Sergeeva (2018).

Sergeeva, E. N. 2018. Markirovanie aktantov dvuxmestnyx predikatov v komi-zyryanskom literaturnom yazyke [Bivalent verbs' argument coding in standard Komi-Zyrian]. In S. S. Say (ed.). Valentnostnye klassy dvuxmestnyx predikatov v raznostrukturnyx jazykax [Bivalent valency classes in structurally diverse languages]. Saint Petersburg: ILS RAN. 339–353.
