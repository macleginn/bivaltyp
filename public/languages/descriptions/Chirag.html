Contributed by Dmitry Ganenkov.

The data were gathered in 2023. The Russian version of the questionnaire was used for elicitation.

How to cite

Ganenkov, Dmitry. 2024. Bivalent patterns in Chirag. In: Say, Sergey (ed.). BivalTyp: Typological database of bivalent verbs and their encoding frames. (Data first published on January 20, 2024; last revised on February 7, 2024.) (Available online at https://bivaltyp.info, Accessed on )

Basic info

  • Coordinates: 41.84, 47.43.
  • Genealogy (as given in WALS). Family: Nakh-Daghestanian, genus: Dargwic.
  • Macro-area: West Asia and the Caucasus.
